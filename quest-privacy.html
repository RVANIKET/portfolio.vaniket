Privacy Policy for Quest - LifeOS

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quest - LifeOS. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Quest - LifeOS is a personal productivity app that includes features such as habit tracking, task management, journaling, and money tracking.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

All data you create and use within the app, including the following, is stored locally on your device only.

  • Habits
  • Tasks
  • Journal entries
  • Financial records

3. Storage Permission

The app may request local storage access for the following purposes:

  • Saving your data on your device
  • Backing up or restoring data (if implemented)
  • Exporting or importing files

We do not access, scan, or upload any unrelated files from your device.

4. Data Usage

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Displaying your information within the app
  • Providing app features and functionality

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:

  • Third-party services
  • Advertisers
  • Analytics platforms

Your data remains fully private and under your control.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally:

  • You are responsible for keeping your device secure.
  • Uninstalling the app may result in permanent data loss unless backup features are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Quest - LifeOS does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected externally, the app is safe for general use.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this section with an updated date.

9. Your Consent

By using Quest - LifeOS, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
