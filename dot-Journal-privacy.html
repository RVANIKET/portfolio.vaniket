Privacy Policy for Dot – Bullet Journal

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Dot – Bullet Journal respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

All data you create within the app, including journal entries, notes, tasks, and bullet journal content, is stored locally on your device only.

3. Local Data Storage

  • All information you create within the app is saved locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your data.
  • If you uninstall the app, your data may be permanently deleted unless you have created a backup.

4. Permissions

The app may request storage access only when needed for backup and restore features.

  • Storage access is used only for saving and restoring backup files.
  • You have full control over where your backup files are stored.
  • We do not access your files without your permission.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

No data is transmitted outside your device.

6. Third-Party Services

Dot – Bullet Journal does not use:

  • Third-party analytics
  • Advertisements
  • User tracking tools

7. Data Security

  • Your data remains on your device.
  • We rely on your device’s built-in security, such as PIN, password, biometrics, and device encryption.
  • We recommend enabling device security for better protection.

8. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Since no data is collected, no personal information is stored or shared by us.

9. Your Control

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Edit or delete entries anytime
  • Create and restore backups manually
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this section with an updated date.
